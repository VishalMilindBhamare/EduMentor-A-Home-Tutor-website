Our Story & Mission

Transforming education through personalized home tutoring since 2015

Who We Are

EduMentor was founded in 2015 with a simple vision: to provide high-quality, personalized education to students in the comfort of their homes.

What began as a small team of passionate educators has now grown into a network of over 800 qualified tutors serving students across 25+ cities. We believe every student deserves individual attention and a learning approach tailored to their unique needs.

Our tutors are not just subject experts but also mentors who inspire confidence and ignite curiosity in their students.
