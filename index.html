Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tutoring services

How do I select the right tutor for my child?

After you submit your requirements, we'll match you with suitable tutors based on your child's needs. You can review tutor profiles, qualifications, and experience before making a selection. We also offer a trial session to ensure the tutor is the right fit.

What are your tutor qualification standards?

All our tutors undergo a rigorous screening process including qualification verification, background checks, and teaching demonstrations. Most hold degrees in their subject areas and have prior teaching experience.

How much does home tutoring cost?

Pricing varies based on the tutor's experience, subject complexity, and grade level. Our rates are competitive and transparent with no hidden fees. Contact us for detailed pricing information based on your specific requirements.

What safety measures do you have for home tutoring?

All our tutors undergo thorough background checks and verification. We maintain complete records of all tutors. For added security, initial sessions can be conducted in common areas of your home or online until you're comfortable.

Can I change tutors if needed?

Absolutely. If for any reason you're not satisfied with your current tutor, we'll help you find a better match at no additional cost. Your child's learning experience is our top priority.
